About Us

Pioneering Mathematical Excellence

The Mathematical Sciences Foundation was established in 2002 by leading Indian citizens, scientists, civil servants, and corporates with a vision to transform mathematical education in India.

Based in Delhi, we are dedicated to promoting mathematics and its applications across all educational levels‚Äîfrom school to college and research. Our innovative learning systems connect mathematics with science, technology, and industry.
