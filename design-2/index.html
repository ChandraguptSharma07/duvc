About Us

Shaping the Future of Mathematical Education

The Mathematical Sciences Foundation was established by leading Indian scientists, civil servants, and corporates with a mission to transform how mathematics is taught and learned.

Based in Delhi, we connect mathematics with science, technology, and industry through innovative learning systems. Our programs span from school students to graduate researchers, and we're pioneering new initiatives like The Internet College and The College of Startups.
